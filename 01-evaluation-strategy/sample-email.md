# Defend Your Trade-off

**Subject:** Why We're Prioritizing Trust Over Raw Response Speed (Latency)

Hi Leo,

I understand the concern that faster responses can improve the user experience and make product demonstrations feel more impressive. However, for Ascend IQ, our primary customers are VP-Level strategists making high-stakes business decisions based on the information we provide.

For these users, a response that arrives one or two seconds faster has far less value than one that includes transparent citations and clearly communicates uncertainty when the available evidence is incomplete. A single unsupported recommendation or fabricated competitor insight could damage customer trust and jeopardize enterprise contracts.

By prioritizing **UX Trust over Latency**, we're optimizing for long-term customer confidence and retention rather than short-term demo appeal. We'll continue improving response times, but not at the expense of the transparency and reliability that differentiate Ascend IQ from generic AI assistants.
